For referring providers

Refer a patient

Thank you for trusting the practice with your patient. Referrals are seen quickly, urgent concerns get same-week appointments, and your office receives a consultation note after every visit.

Fax referrals to (949) 687-1217 or call (714) 919-8143 to coordinate directly.
01

What to send

Patient demographics and insurance, your notes for the relevant problem, and any recent PSA, labs, or imaging. Incomplete records are never a barrier; send what you have.

02

Scheduling

The office calls your patient directly. Urgent referrals, including acute stones and retention, are seen the same week. Telehealth is available when travel is hard.

03

Closing the loop

You receive a consultation note after the visit and updates at each decision point, so your patient never disappears into a specialty black box.

Patients who do especially well here

Bothersome BPH

Men failing or tired of medical therapy. The practice offers PAE, UroLift, Rezūm, and Aquablation, so the recommendation fits the anatomy rather than the available tool.

Anticoagulated or higher-risk men

PAE requires no general anesthesia and no interruption of blood thinners for many patients, a practical answer for cardiac and elderly patients.

Very large prostates

PAE and Aquablation both work across the full size range, including glands where office therapies fall short.

Stones, cancer, and men's health

Same-week stone care, robotic prostate and kidney surgery at Providence St. Joseph, and a complete men's health program.
