How treatment works

Testosterone naturally declines with age, but when levels fall low enough they affect energy, libido, mood, muscle, and sleep. The diagnosis is straightforward: symptoms plus morning blood levels, confirmed on repeat testing before any treatment begins.

When low testosterone is confirmed, therapy is tailored to you and your goals, and levels are rechecked on a regular schedule. The aim is a healthy physiologic range, better energy and function without overshooting, with attention to fertility and prostate health along the way.
