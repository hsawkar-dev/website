How it works

The vas deferens are the two tubes that carry sperm. A vasectomy interrupts them so sperm can no longer reach the semen. The no-scalpel technique reaches those tubes through a single tiny opening, rather than the incisions of a traditional vasectomy.

The whole procedure takes about 15 to 30 minutes, right in the office, and you go home the same day. A follow-up semen analysis confirms when the vasectomy is fully effective.
