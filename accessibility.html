Accessibility

This site is built to be usable by everyone: semantic headings, keyboard-reachable controls, a skip-to-content link, alternative text on meaningful images, and reduced-motion support for visitors who prefer less animation.

If any part of the site is difficult for you to use, or you need this information in another format, call the office at (714) 919-8143 and the team will help directly. Feedback about accessibility is genuinely welcome and gets fixed.

The office itself is wheelchair accessible, with free parking throughout the medical building complex.
