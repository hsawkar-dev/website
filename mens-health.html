Men's Health

Beyond "Just Getting Older"

Erectile dysfunction, low testosterone, and changes in sexual function are common — but they are not simply inevitable parts of aging. These are medical conditions with identifiable causes and highly effective treatments.

Dr. Sawkar takes a comprehensive, root-cause approach to men's sexual and hormonal health. Rather than masking symptoms, the goal is to identify the underlying issue — whether vascular, hormonal, neurological, or structural — and address it directly.

All consultations are handled with complete discretion and sensitivity. Many men wait years before seeking care; Dr. Sawkar's practice provides a welcoming, non-judgmental environment to have these conversations.

ED as a Health Signal: Erectile dysfunction is often one of the earliest warning signs of cardiovascular disease, diabetes, or hormonal imbalance — frequently appearing years before other symptoms. A proper evaluation doesn't just restore function; it can uncover conditions that benefit your overall health.

Erectile Dysfunction

Regenerative Treatments — Restore Function, Not Just Symptoms

Modern ED care goes beyond pills and injections. Dr. Sawkar offers regenerative therapies that target the underlying vascular and tissue damage causing ED — restoring natural erectile function rather than providing temporary workarounds.

Platelet Rich Plasma (PRP) Injections

The P-Shot

PRP therapy uses concentrated growth factors drawn from your own blood to stimulate tissue repair, collagen regeneration, and new blood vessel formation within the erectile tissue of the penis. By addressing the cellular and vascular damage that underlies ED, PRP produces lasting, structural improvement in erectile function — not just a temporary response.

Because PRP is autologous — derived from your own blood — there is no risk of allergic reaction or rejection. The procedure is performed in-office in under an hour, with no downtime required.

Key Benefits
  • Uses your own blood — no foreign substances
  • Stimulates tissue regeneration and neovascularization
  • In-office procedure, minimal downtime
  • Can be combined with shockwave therapy for enhanced outcomes

Low Intensity Shockwave Therapy

Li-SWT

Low intensity shockwave therapy delivers focused acoustic waves to penile tissue, stimulating angiogenesis — the growth of new blood vessels — and clearing micro-plaque from existing vessels. This directly treats the vascular insufficiency that underlies the majority of ED cases.

Clinical studies demonstrate durable improvements in erectile function, with many men reducing or eliminating their need for PDE5 inhibitors (Viagra, Cialis) entirely. Treatment consists of a short series of in-office sessions with no anesthesia, no downtime, and no side effects.

Key Benefits
  • Non-invasive — no anesthesia, no downtime
  • Addresses the root-cause vascular insufficiency
  • Series of brief in-office sessions
  • Durable results not reliant on ongoing medication

Erectile Dysfunction

Additional ED Treatment Options

For men who need additional support beyond regenerative therapies, a full spectrum of options is available — from oral medications to surgical implants. Dr. Sawkar will recommend the approach that best matches your health status, goals, and preferences.

Oral Medications

PDE5 inhibitors (sildenafil, tadalafil, vardenafil) are effective for many men. Available as on-demand or daily dosing, and can be used alongside regenerative therapies for enhanced results.

Intracavernous Injection Therapy

Medication injected directly into the corpora cavernosa provides a reliable erection within minutes. Highly effective even in men who don't respond to oral medications, and can serve as a bridge while regenerative therapies take effect.

Inflatable Penile Implant

The gold standard for men who haven't responded to other treatments. An internally concealed, hydraulic device that produces a completely natural-feeling erection on demand. The highest patient and partner satisfaction rates of any ED treatment — over 90%.

Hormone Health

Testosterone & Hormone Replacement Therapy

Low testosterone (hypogonadism) affects a significant portion of men over 40 — and its symptoms are frequently dismissed as "just getting older." Dr. Sawkar evaluates testosterone and related hormones comprehensively and offers individualized, evidence-based replacement strategies.

Symptoms of Low Testosterone

  • Persistent fatigue and low energy despite adequate sleep
  • Decreased libido and sexual desire
  • Mood changes, irritability, or depression
  • Difficulty building or maintaining muscle mass
  • Brain fog, difficulty concentrating
  • Increased body fat, reduced bone density

Treatment plans are tailored to each patient's goals — including fertility considerations, since some TRT options affect sperm production and are adjusted accordingly.

Treatment Options

Testosterone Injections

Intramuscular or subcutaneous injections — the most common form of TRT, with predictable dosing and straightforward monitoring. Typically administered every 1–2 weeks.

Topical Gels & Creams

Applied daily to skin for stable, consistent testosterone levels without injections. Well-tolerated and easy to adjust dosing. Requires care around skin-to-skin transfer.

Subcutaneous Pellets

Small pellets inserted under the skin every 3–6 months. Provides the most consistent hormone levels with no daily administration — ideal for those who prefer a hands-off approach.

Men's Health

Other Men's Health Concerns

Dr. Sawkar treats the full range of men's sexual and urologic health concerns — providing expert care for conditions that are often undertreated due to stigma or lack of awareness.

Low Libido

Decreased sex drive can stem from low testosterone, medications, stress, relationship dynamics, or other factors. A thorough evaluation identifies the cause — often more than one — and guides an effective, targeted treatment plan.

Peyronie's Disease

Scar tissue (plaque) forms within the tunica albuginea of the penis, causing curvature, pain, and difficulty with intercourse. Treatment options include Xiaflex (collagenase) injections, traction therapy, or surgical correction — depending on severity and whether the condition is in the acute or chronic phase.

Men's Health Screening

A comprehensive men's health evaluation includes PSA, full testosterone and hormonal panel, metabolic workup, and cardiovascular risk assessment. Proactive screening catches problems early — when they are most treatable — and provides a clear picture of your baseline health.
