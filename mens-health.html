Erectile dysfunction

Root-cause care

Rather than only masking symptoms, Dr. Sawkar treats the vascular and tissue causes of ED, with options that range from regenerative therapy to medication and implants.

Focused acoustic waves stimulate new blood vessel growth and clear micro-plaque, treating the vascular root of ED. A short series of in-office sessions, with no anesthesia and no downtime.
