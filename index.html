BPH Treatment

UroLift — No Cutting, No Drugs, No Catheter

The UroLift System is an FDA-cleared, office-based solution for men suffering from lower urinary tract symptoms due to an enlarged prostate (BPH). Small implants lift and hold the prostate tissue away from the urethra — restoring flow within minutes.

UroLift Center of Excellence Nationally recognized for superior patient outcomes
  • Performed in-office, typically under 1 hour
  • No general anesthesia required for most patients
  • Preserves sexual function
  • Most men return to normal activity within days
Learn About UroLift

Rapid Symptom Relief

Clinical studies show significant improvement in urinary symptoms within 2 weeks of the procedure.

No Ongoing Medications

Unlike alpha-blockers and 5-alpha reductase inhibitors, UroLift addresses the mechanical obstruction directly.

Proven Long-Term Results

5-year durability data showing sustained improvement in urinary flow and quality of life.

No Surgery. No Cuts to the Prostate.

PAE is performed entirely through a small puncture in the wrist or groin — the prostate itself is never cut or entered.

Effective for Very Large Prostates

Unlike some office procedures, PAE is an excellent option for men with significantly enlarged prostates who are not good surgical candidates.

Sexual Function Fully Preserved

PAE carries essentially zero risk of retrograde ejaculation or erectile dysfunction — a key advantage over traditional surgical approaches.

PAE — The Non-Surgical BPH Solution

Prostatic Artery Embolization (PAE) shrinks an enlarged prostate by reducing its blood supply through a minimally invasive, image-guided catheter procedure. No general anesthesia. No hospital stay. Back to normal within days.

  • Performed under local anesthesia — no general anesthesia required
  • Outpatient procedure — go home the same day
  • Ideal for men who failed medications or want to avoid surgery
  • Works for very large prostates where other options may not
